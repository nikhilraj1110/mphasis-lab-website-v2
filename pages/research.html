Research

Our Research

25+ projects across five pillars — from AI-driven drug discovery and India-scale epidemic modelling to post-quantum cryptography and digital fabrication.

AI@Ashoka

Artificial intelligence and machine learning applied across health, biodiversity, education, policy, and materials science. Spanning Computer Science, Biology, Chemistry, Environmental Studies, Physics, Psychology, and the Centre for Writing and Communication (CWC).

14 projects 17 faculty

Cancer Ascend

Debayan Gupta & Shubhasis Haldar

OncoMark framework published in Communications Biology (Nature portfolio) — first tool to predict all 10 cancer hallmarks concurrently, with high accuracy across multiple cancer types and independent validation cohorts.

Dengue NS3 Protease Inhibitors

Sourav Chatterjee & Rintu Kutum

Screened millions of molecules using ensemble ML and identified promising compounds as potential dengue NS3 protease inhibitors. A lead compound demonstrated significant viral inhibition in experimental validation with IISER Kolkata.

AI-Driven Antibiotic Discovery

Imroze Khan

Discovering antimicrobial peptides from archaic hominin genomes — identified ~10,000 AMPs from 11 lineages including Neanderthal and Denisovan. 178 peptides validated via molecular dynamics, 4 synthesized for in-vitro screening against multidrug-resistant pathogens.

AI-Powered Biomarker Discovery

Kasturi Mitra

Using AI and cancer cell biology to predict mitochondrial redox function from structural features. Machine learning models demonstrated strong predictive performance across experimental groups, with a meta-learning approach generalizing effectively to unseen data.

AI-Driven Demographic Inference

Kritika Garg & Balaji Chattopadhyay

ML pipeline for inferring demographic history from genetic data. Validated on real-world Siamese crocodile genetic data, successfully predicting a documented population bottleneck.

AIM-Flow

Rama Akondy

AI tools for acute myeloid leukemia diagnosis from flow cytometry data. Validated on public datasets with strong classification performance across patient cohorts.

Infant Gaze Pattern Analysis

Madhavi Maganti

Screening infant neurodevelopmental risk using AI analysis of gaze patterns at Dr. RML Hospital, New Delhi. Hundreds of infants assessed using a structured clinical framework by neonatologists, with ML-based age classification models developed.

High-Throughput COF Screening

Vidya Avasare

AI-accelerated discovery of Covalent Organic Frameworks for carbon dioxide capture. Large-scale computational screening pipeline developed with hundreds of optimized structures and a curated experimental database.

High-Resolution Fire Mapping

Meghna Agarwala

High-resolution AI models for fire detection using satellite imagery. 35 students trained on GIS, remote sensing, and ML. Presented at NASA-SARI meeting. Wall-to-wall fire product code finalized for Central India.

AI Detection of Pollen

Meghna Agarwala

AI-powered pollen identification for paleo-ecology. Pollen library calibrated for 12 taxa with 40+ species from Satpura Tiger Reserve. Robotic microscope attachment developed with Digital Makerspace. PI awarded Schmidt AI for Science Fellowship at Oxford.

Acoustic Biodiversity Monitoring

Bittu Kaveri Rajaraman

Automating biodiversity assessment through acoustic monitoring of ground insect species in Assam. ~120 hours of audio data collected over 55+ recording days in Bihpuria, North Lakhimpur District.

Adaptive & Inclusive CS Pedagogy

Aalok Thakkar

BOOP (VS Code extension for formal methods) and SocraticAI (guardrailed LLM for scaffolded learning) deployed across 2 semesters at Ashoka. 4 papers at ACM COMPUTE 2025. SocraticAI featured as a case study in MeitY's Compendium on the Real-World Impact of AI in Education.

Assistive AI for Academic Writing (CWC)

Neerav Dwivedi, Sampurna Dutta & Archishman Sarker

Assessed AI tools for argumentation in academic writing through a controlled experiment with 26 participants across 5 disciplines. Report launched February 2026 with an AI & Academic Integrity Workshop (34 attendees).

IndiaMediaLens

Anirban Sen & Debayan Gupta

Two live systems for media monitoring: a News Adjudication Monitor dashboard and a Chrome browser plugin with bias indicators. Analyses thousands of articles from 20+ sources alongside decades of Press Council data.

BharatSim

Distributed, multi-scale agent-based simulation framework for epidemic modelling and population health. International media coverage across 20+ outlets including BBC.

1 project 10+ invited talks

BharatSim Agent-Based Modelling

Gautam Menon & Debayan Gupta

H5N1 avian influenza modelling paper published in BMC Public Health, covered by BBC, NDTV, CNBC-TV18, Nature India, and 20+ international outlets. Presentations at Wellcome Trust (London), WHO-SEARO, Border Security Force Academy, IIM Kozhikode, and more.

CHART

Centre for Health Analytics Research and Trends — AI at the intersection of healthcare, food systems, and translational medicine. Organized the first International Workshop on Multimodal Food Computing at ACM Multimedia 2025.

3 projects 8+ publications

Folk Computing for Abdominal Pain

Rintu Kutum

PRISM (Physician Rules Integrated with Small LLMs) for conversational diagnosis of abdominal pain, accepted at NeurIPS 2025 GenAI4Health Workshop. Supports Hindi voice interaction for accessible clinical use.

Multimodal Food Computing

Lipika Dey & Partha Pratim Das

Multiple peer-reviewed publications. Organized MMFood'25 at ACM Multimedia in Dublin. SmartDiet won the Promising Innovation Award at INFUSE Innovation Summit 2025. Food Composition Table expanded to 10,000+ recipes. Indian Recipe Dialogue System (IRDS) deployed with voice interaction and vernacular language support.

Triple Negative Breast Cancer (TNBC)

L.S. Shashidhara

Profiled 100+ TNBC patients across 9 actionable molecular markers. Presented at EACR 2025 in Lisbon. Clinical data curated from nearly 5,000 breast cancer cases. Key finding: patients with high tumour-infiltrating lymphocytes showed significantly better survival outcomes.

Cybersecurity

Post-quantum cryptography research developing DNS security protocols resilient against quantum computing threats. Published at ACM ASIACCS 2025.

1 project 4 publications

Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC)

Mahavir Jhawar

Presented at ACM ASIACCS 2025 (Hanoi). Research spans post-quantum DNS security protocols, lattice-based cryptographic foundations, and network security analysis.

Makerspace

Digital fabrication lab, scientific instrumentation, student innovation hub, and enabling platform for research across all pillars. Publications in Nature portfolio and Proceedings of the Royal Society B.

4 projects 2,000+ K-12 students

Digital Makerspace

Deepraj Pandey, Sai Khurana & Kanishk Singh

Innovation hub reaching ~1,450 students and 2,000+ school students. Major equipment includes laser cutters, CNC routers, industrial 3D printers, and drones. Hosted RedBrick Hacks III (430+ applicants from 91 universities).

Covalent Magnetic Tweezers (CMT)

Debayan Gupta & Shubhasis Haldar

Built a complete single-molecule force spectroscopy instrument — described as "unique in India." Published in Communications Biology (Nature), Protein Science, and Biochemistry. Awarded Cell Press Technology of the Month.

Table-Top Glacier Flow Dynamics

Pramoda Kumar

Table-top fluid experiments studying glacier flow dynamics. Controlled experiments confirmed self-similar flow profiles. Analysis of fingering instability developed. 3rd place at Meera Memorial Competition.

Pedagogical Physics Experiments

Pramoda Kumar

Quantum optics and statistical physics experimental setups designed and built for the Physics department curriculum, enabling hands-on learning with advanced instrumentation.
