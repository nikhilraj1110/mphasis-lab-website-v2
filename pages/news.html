Flagship Event

RedBrick Hacks III

A national student hackathon — February 6–8, 2026, Ashoka University

0+

Challenge Tracks

RedBrick Hacks is the Lab's annual student hackathon, bringing together students from across India to build solutions for real-world challenges. The third edition featured four challenge tracks: Quality Education, Climate Action, Sustainable Cities, and Hardware.

After a month-long online submission phase, 60 finalists were invited to Ashoka University for 48 hours of building, mentoring, and pitching. Notable guests included Prof. Partha Pratim Chakrabarti (former Director, IIT Kharagpur) and Kailash Nadh (CTO, Zerodha).

1,300+ total applications across all three editions of RedBrick Hacks.
