India-Scale Epidemic Simulation

Agent-based modelling of a nation of 1.4 billion individuals, simulating disease dynamics at unprecedented scale. The BharatSim framework enables policy-relevant scenario analysis for public health decision-making across India’s diverse demographic landscape.
