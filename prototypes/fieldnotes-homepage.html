Five Pillars of Innovation

Our research spans five interconnected pillars, each addressing critical challenges through technology and interdisciplinary collaboration.

AI@Ashoka

Advancing artificial intelligence research across education, health, environment, humanities, and fundamental sciences.

BharatSim

India-scale agent-based simulation framework for epidemiological modeling and public health policy planning.

CHART

Centre for Health Analytics Research and Trends — clinical AI, food computing, and cancer diagnostics.

Cybersecurity

Post-quantum cryptography research to develop encryption systems resilient against quantum computing threats.

Makerspace

Digital fabrication, maker education, and hands-on innovation powering interdisciplinary research and student projects.
