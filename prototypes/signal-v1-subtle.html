◆ BharatSim | BMC Public Health · 2025

India-Scale Epidemic Simulation

Agent-based modelling of a nation. Our H5N1 avian flu study was covered by BBC, NDTV, Nature India, and 20+ international outlets. The platform is now deployed to the National AIDS Control Organization for HIV predictive modelling.
