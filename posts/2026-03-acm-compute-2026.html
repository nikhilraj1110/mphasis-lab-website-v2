ACM COMPUTE 2026 Regional Workshop at Ashoka University

A day of talks and hands-on sessions on CS education, hosted by the Department of Computer Science.

Ashoka University hosted the ACM COMPUTE 2026 Regional Workshop on March 21, 2026. The event was organised by Prof. Aalok Thakkar (Department of Computer Science, Ashoka University) along with Prajish Prasad (FLAME University) and other collaborators.

ACM COMPUTE is a premier computing conference series in India. The regional workshop included a discussion forum on computer science education and two hands-on workshop sessions with 10 to 12 participants each.

Programme

  • A discussion forum on computer science education themes, with educators and researchers from multiple institutions
  • Two hands-on workshop sessions focused on CS education tools and approaches
